Gulberg Residensia — 5, 7 & 10 Marla

Dream Built Construction has delivered multiple residential house projects across Gulberg Residensia, spanning 5, 7, and 10 Marla plot sizes — from classical Mughal-inspired designs with white balustraded facades to contemporary reinforced concrete frame structures. Each home was constructed to the client's specification with Dream Built managing all phases from foundation through to complete finishing.
